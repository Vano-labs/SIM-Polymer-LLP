ABOUT US
SIM Polymers LLP
SIM POLYMERS LLP, INCORPORATION began its successful journey as a prominent raw material supplying company in the year 2019. Since its inception, SIM POLYMERS LLP has grown by leaps and bounds due to its competent services as a raw material sourcing unit, high-quality standards and an inspired management that is committed to deliver nothing but excellence. With a portfolio that finds its application across several industries, SIM POLYMERS LLP is your one-stop shop for all things POLYMER. Having started humbly, with just a few locally procured small-scale products in the inventory, the company has progressed manifold and now imports raw materials from some of the finest establishments worldwide – USA, Qatar, Oman, Vietnam and several other countries. With a supply chain across several premier industries, the company is now poised for firm growth and development and thereby aims to strategically strengthen its foothold across markets in India and abroad
